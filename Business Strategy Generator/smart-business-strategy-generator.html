What frameworks does the Strategy Generator use?+

It generates a SWOT analysis (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats), a Business Model Canvas, and a 90-day growth roadmap tailored to your inputs.

Can I export my strategy?+

Yes. You can export your complete strategy as a PDF document to share with your team or investors.
