About SummCore

Where imagination meets possibility meets reality.

What is SummCore?

SummCore is an innovation consulting firm that helps small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) and startups turn ideas into measurable growth. We combine hands-on consulting with a growing suite of free, no-sign-up business tools that cover everything from financial health checks and strategy generation to revenue forecasting and invoicing.

Our approach is practical: we build the tools we wish existed when growing a business, then give them away for free. Consulting is how we go deeper, working one-to-one with founders and teams to solve the problems no template can fix.

What does SummCore offer?

Free Business Tools

Six interactive tools covering business health checks, strategy generation (SWOT, BMC, 90-day roadmaps), financial analysis, revenue forecasting, invoicing, and business plan templates. All free, all browser-based, no account required.

Innovation Consulting

One-to-one consulting for SMEs and startups. We work with founders on idea generation, revenue strategy, process improvement, product development, and ongoing support to turn plans into results.

AI-Powered Assessments

SummCore Boost delivers in-depth business health assessments powered by AI. Answer 26 questions across five pillars and receive a scored report with prioritised recommendations.

Growth Strategy Frameworks

From SWOT analyses and Business Model Canvases to 90-day growth roadmaps, we give SMEs access to the same strategic frameworks used by larger firms, without the price tag.

The SummCore Ecosystem

SummCore is more than a single website. We are building a connected ecosystem of tools and platforms designed to support businesses at every stage of growth.
