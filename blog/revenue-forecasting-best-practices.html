Revenue Forecasting Best Practices

By SummCore | Last updated: February 2026

Forecasts guide hiring, inventory, and marketing. Here’s how to make them credible and useful.

How do you choose the right forecasting model?

Top-down for TAM narratives, bottom-up for operational planning, and cohort models for subscriptions.

Why should you make assumptions explicit?

Document drivers: conversion rates, CAC, churn, and price changes. Track actuals vs plan monthly.
