Product-Led Growth for Service Businesses

By SummCore | Last updated: February 2026

Service firms can adopt PLG by productizing offers, simplifying onboarding, and showcasing value early.

How do you create a productized offer?

Bundle scope, price, and process into a clear plan with a frictionless checkout or booking.

How do you measure activation and retention?

Track activation moments (first deliverable, first strategy session) and build retention loops.
