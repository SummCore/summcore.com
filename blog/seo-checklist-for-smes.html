SEO Checklist for SMEs: 2025 Edition

By SummCore | Last updated: February 2026

Use this practical checklist to optimize your site for search in 2025.

What are the on-page SEO basics?

  • Unique title and meta description per page
  • Single H1, semantic H2/H3 structure
  • Descriptive alt text and lazy-loading for images

What technical SEO should SMEs prioritise?

  • Robots.txt and XML sitemap
  • Canonical links and lowercase keyword URLs
  • Core Web Vitals: minified CSS/JS, responsive design

How should SMEs use schema markup and content strategy?

  • JSON-LD for Organization, Article, Product or SoftwareApplication
  • Internal linking with descriptive anchors
  • Topic clusters and consistent publishing cadence
